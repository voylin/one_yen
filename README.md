# One Yen

[![Discord](https://img.shields.io/discord/1120566516899926068?label=Discord&logo=discord&logoColor=white)](https://discord.gg/BdbUf7VKYC)

_A financial tracker made with Godot._

<img src="./src/icon.svg" alt="One Yen Logo" width="200"/>

With One Yen you can track your income and expenses without having to worry that bigger companies are going to use that data. Everything stay's on your system and yours alone.

## Download
You can get the downloads from the [release tab](https://github.com/voylin/one_yen/releases) or from [the Itch.io page](https://voylin.itch.io/one-yen).

## Why made with Godot?
Godot is an easy to use game engine, which is great for making UI applications in a way that people with limited knowledge can pick up, understand, and adjust the project to their specific needs.

One Yen isn't a fancy money tracker, there's no AI, there's no connectivity between different pc's, and there's no data tracking from remote servers to spy on you. Just you and One Yen to track your income and expenses.
